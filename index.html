Tolle Lege

“Take up and read” — a 3D Bible study tool.

Click any word to study the Greek or Hebrew behind it. Drag across words to select a passage. Click a rubric verse number for its cross-references. Place names are inked in lapis blue — click one to see it on the map. The reference rows in the study panel turn the book's pages to that passage.

Texts & data

  • Scripture text and word-level Greek/Hebrew alignment: Berean Standard Bible, dedicated to the public domain.
  • Strong's Hebrew and Greek dictionaries: Open Scriptures (CC BY-SA 4.0).
  • Cross-references: OpenBible.info (CC BY), derived from the Treasury of Scripture Knowledge.
  • Place locations: OpenBible.info Bible Geocoding (CC BY 4.0).
  • Maps rendered with Leaflet; imagery tiles © Esri — Esri, Maxar, Earthstar Geographics.
  • Rendered with Three.js (WebGPU).
